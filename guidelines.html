Guidelines

To foster safe, nurturing, and comfortable cuddle sessions, please follow the standard cuddling guidelines below.

Inclusivity Commitment: I welcome people of all races, cultures, nationalities, sexual orientations, gender identities, (dis)abilities, neurotypes, body types, ages over 18, belief systems, and socioeconomic backgrounds. My practice is rooted in respect, compassion, and the belief that everyone deserves safe and affirming human connection.

  1. You must be 18 or older to cuddle.

  2. We both agree to be free of mind-altering substances and therefore fully able to consent to cuddling.

  3. We both agree to openly communicate what is mutually comfortable throughout the session.

  4. We both agree to practice consent and respect each other's personal boundaries at all times.

  5. This is a strictly platonic service and we both agree to not pursue or encourage sexual arousal.
    • Minimum clothing is a tank top and shorts.
    • No touching of genitals, breasts, or buttocks.
    • No intentional genital stimulation, grinding, or humping.
    • Absolutely no kissing of any part of the body.

  6. We both agree to maintain good hygiene and wear clean clothes for the session.

  7. Your confidentiality will be respected unless a safety issue arises (e.g. sexualized behaviour).

  8. You may end the session at any time for any reason, and I may pause or end the session if you are not following these guidelines.

  9. We both agree to respect each other's time and begin sessions at the scheduled time.
    • If you are running late, please text me to let me know. My timer will begin at the scheduled time and you will still be responsible to pay for the full session that was booked, unless there are exceptional circumstances that warrant a compassionate price adjustment.
    • If I am running behind, I will not start my timer until we begin, as a courtesy to ensure you still get the full time you have booked. Alternatively, if you need to leave at the original end time, we can save the missed time to be added to your next session.

  10. Cancellations require 24 hours' notice to ensure no charge for the missed session.
    • Unexplained no-shows or cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice will only be accepted for future bookings once the missed session is paid in full, unless there are exceptional circumstances that warrant a compassionate waiving of this policy.
    • If I cancel with less than 24 hours' notice, you will receive half off your next same-length session.
